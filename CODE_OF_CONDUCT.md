# Code of Conduct

dashd is a small project. The expectation here is simple: **treat everyone with respect.**

## Examples of acceptable behaviour

- Assume the other person is acting in good faith.
- Disagree with ideas, not people.
- Welcome questions from people new to the project, the tools, or the platform — everyone was new once.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.

## Examples of unacceptable behaviour

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Harassment, public or private — including persistent unwanted contact.
- Discrimination based on race, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, age, religion, nationality, or any other protected characteristic.
- Publishing other people's private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussion.

## Scope

This applies to every project space — issues, pull requests, code comments, commit messages, the discussions tab — and to any public communication where you're representing dashd.

## Reporting

Open behaviour concerns are handled by the maintainer, Cristian Onescu (crisonescu@gmail.com). Reports are kept confidential. Responses range from a private note through to permanent removal from the project, depending on severity and context.
